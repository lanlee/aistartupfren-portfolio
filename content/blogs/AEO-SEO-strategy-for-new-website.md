You can transform your AI startup's visibility in just 48 hours. Here's how:

## Hour 0-8: Research & Foundation

- Identify your target keywords
- Analyze competitor content
- Map out your content structure

## Hour 8-16: Content Creation

- Write 5-10 high-quality articles
- Use AI tools to optimize for AEO/GEO
- Add schema markup

## Hour 16-24: Technical Optimization

- Improve site speed
- Add proper meta tags
- Set up sitemap and robots.txt

## Hour 24-32: Distribution

- Share on social media
- Post in relevant communities
- Email your list

## Hour 32-40: Backlink Building

- Reach out for guest posts
- Comment on relevant blogs
- Create shareable assets

## Hour 40-48: Monitoring & Iteration

- Track rankings
- Analyze traffic
- Plan next steps

**This isn't a one-time thing - it's a sprint to get started. Then you iterate weekly.**
